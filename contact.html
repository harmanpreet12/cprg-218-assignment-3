Frequently Asked Questions

What is your check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time is at 3:00 PM and check-out time is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability for an additional fee.

How do I get to the resort from the airport?

We offer complimentary airport transfers for all our guests. Please provide your flight details at least 48 hours prior to arrival, and our staff will be waiting to welcome you at the airport.

Do you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Yes, we cater to various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform us of any special dietary needs at the time of booking or contact our guest services team prior to your arrival.

What activities are included in the stay?

Complimentary activities include access to our infinity pool, fitness center, beach volleyball, sunrise yoga, and meditation sessions. Additional activities such as snorkeling, diving, and excursions are available for a fee.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days or more prior to arrival receive a full refund minus a processing fee. Cancellations made 15-29 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 15 days before arrival are non-refundable.
